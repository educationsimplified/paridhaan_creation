SHIPPING, RETURNS & EXCHANGES POLICY

We want you to be absolutely delighted with your purchase from us. Should your order not meet your expectations, we are pleased to offer exchanges and returns for all items purchased from Paridhaan Creation, provided the following conditions are met:

  • ✓ All items must be in their original packaging with product tags intact.
  • ✓ All items must be unworn, unused, unwashed, and in their original condition.
  • ✓ The original receipt must be included.
  • ✓ All items must have been purchased exclusively from our website.

Should any of these conditions not be met, we regret to inform you that we are unable to process any claims for exchanges or refunds, even if the items have been returned to us. Please note that sale items are non-exchangeable and non-returnable.

1. SHIPPING POLICY

All orders are dispatched within 2 working days from our facility. Deliveries within India are estimated to arrive within 4-7 days depending on your location, while worldwide deliveries may take 15-30 days.

2. VALIDITY FOR EXCHANGES & RETURNS

All requests for exchanges and returns must be initiated within 10 days of the invoice date for domestic orders and 20 days for overseas orders. For our valued customers in Varanasi, we offer the convenience of exchanging items at our local boutiques or via our online portal at varanasibrand.com. All return requests, however, must be strictly processed online for both local and international orders.

3. EXCHANGES AT OUR BOUTIQUES

  • 3.1 Please bring your original invoice along with the item(s) in their original condition and packaging to any of our boutiques.
  • 3.2 Following a quick evaluation by our retail associates to confirm validity, you will be entitled to an immediate exchange for any available item(s) in the boutique.
  • 3.3 Should the value of the exchanged item(s) exceed the original value, you will be required to pay the difference.
  • 3.4 Please note that we do not provide refunds if the value of the exchanged item(s) is lower than the original.
  • 3.5 No other forms of exchange, such as boutique cash vouchers or online store credit, will be issued at our physical locations.

4. EXCHANGES & RETURNS ONLINE

  • 4.1 You will need to include your original invoice with the item(s) in their original condition and packaging.
  • 4.2 We kindly request that you mail your parcel back to us using a traceable postage method.
  • 4.3 Upon receipt of your parcel, we will send you an email confirmation and notify you once your online Store Credit Voucher is ready. The processing period is typically 5 working days.
  • 4.4 All returns will be refunded exclusively via an online Store Credit Voucher. This voucher is valid for 30 days and is to be used for your next online purchase. It is strictly non-refundable, non-extendable, non-exchangeable for cash, and cannot be used in our retail boutiques.
  • 4.5 Online Store Credit Vouchers are for a single transaction. Any unused amount will not be refunded.
  • 4.6 The online Store Credit Voucher will reflect the original buying price of the product as shown on your invoice. We do not provide refunds for any delivery charges incurred.
  • 4.7 In the unfortunate event that you receive a defective or incorrect item, we will happily cover the cost of shipping the replacement item(s) back to you. All exchanges are subject to stock availability, and we reserve the right to provide an item of similar value or online store credits at our discretion.
  • 4.8 Refunds will not be provided for taxes, duties, tariffs, or excise charges levied on overseas orders.
  • 4.9 All online returns and exchanges should be shipped to the following address:
